Our Story

Six Decades of Trust.
A New Era of Innovation.

FABREVOL represents the future of chemical logistics, built on a foundation of historical trust. Born from the prestigious Rangwala Group, which has been pioneering chemical distribution since 1958 in Rajasthan, FABREVOL was established in Pune in 2022 by Mr. Yash Jhanwar and Mr. Rajendra Jhanwar.

Today, we combine decades of ancestral expertise with next-generation supply chain agility to revolutionize chemical distribution across Maharashtra and Pan India.
