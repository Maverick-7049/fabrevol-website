Category 04

Paints & Coating Additives

Specialty additives for water-borne, solvent-borne, and solvent-free paint systems, enhancing performance during manufacturing, storage, and post-application at typical loadings of 0.01%–5%.

Rheology Modifiers

HEC · HEUR · HASE Thickeners
Cellulosic & Synthetic Associative Types

Prevents pigment settling during storage and sagging on vertical surfaces post-application. Improves in-can consistency, brush-ability, flow, levelling, and spatter resistance during roller application.

Dispersing & Wetting Agents

High-MW Polyacrylates · Block Copolymers · Non-ionic Surfactants
Steric & Electrostatic Stabilization

Prevents pigment flocculation and agglomeration. Drastically improves colour development, tinting strength, and gloss. Reduces dynamic surface tension to prevent crawling and cratering over difficult or contaminated substrates.

Defoamers & De-aerators

Silicone Oils · Modified Polysiloxanes · Mineral Oil + Hydrophobic Silica
For grinding, let-down, spray & roller application

Rapidly breaks macro-foam during pigment grinding and micro-foam (entrapped air) formed during spray or roller application. Prevents surface defects including pinholing and fisheyes in the final film.

Matting Agents

Precipitated Synthetic Silica · PE Wax · Polyamide Wax
Untreated & Wax-treated Grades Available

Micro-roughens the dry coating film to diffuse incident light. Delivers efficient, consistent gloss reduction to achieve matte, satin, or eggshell finishes without compromising transparency or film toughness.

Adhesion Promoters

Organo-functional Silanes · Chlorinated Polyolefins (CPO) · Acrylic Resins
For glass, aluminium, galvanised steel, PP/PE

Acts as a molecular bridge between the organic coating binder and inorganic or difficult substrates. Ensures long-term adhesion and moisture resistance on low-surface-energy plastics and non-porous metals.

UV Absorbers & Light Stabilizers (HALS)

Benzotriazoles · Triazines · Benzophenones · Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers
0.1%–2% loading in exterior coatings & outdoor plastics

UVA acts as a molecular sunblock, absorbing harmful UV radiation and dissipating it as heat. HALS scavenges free radicals formed by photo-oxidation, breaking the polymer degradation cycle. Prevents chalking, cracking, embrittlement, and pigment fading.

Exterior Architectural Coatings Automotive Clearcoats Wood Finishes Plastic Masterbatches

Construction Chemicals & Water Repellents

Alkyl alkoxysilanes, oligomeric siloxanes, and silane/siloxane micro-emulsions providing deep-penetrating, breathable hydrophobic protection for concrete, masonry, and natural stone.

Aqueous Water Repellent
